# How to Reserve a Table at Kumar Restaurant: Complete Booking Guide

Planning a visit to Kumar Restaurant? Whether it's your first time or your fiftieth, making a reservation ensures you get a table when you want one. This guide walks you through every way to book, what to expect, and how to make the most of your dining experience.

## Why Make a Reservation?

Kumar Restaurant is a popular dining destination in Hamamatsu, and tables fill up—especially during dinner service, weekends, and the lunch rush on weekdays. While walk-ins are welcome during all operating hours, a reservation guarantees your table and lets us prepare for your arrival.

Reservations are particularly recommended for:

- **Friday and Saturday evenings** — Our busiest times
- **Lunch service on weekdays** — The business lunch crowd arrives early
- **Holiday periods** — Golden Week, Obon, New Year's
- **Groups of 4 or more** — Larger parties benefit from reserved seating
- **Special occasions** — Birthdays, anniversaries, celebrations

## How to Book

We offer several convenient ways to reserve your table:

**Online Reservation**

Our online booking system is the fastest way to secure your table. You can make a reservation 24/7, select your preferred date and time, and receive instant confirmation.

**Phone Reservation**

If you prefer to speak with someone directly, call us during business hours. Our staff can answer questions about the menu, recommend dishes, and help plan special requests.

**WhatsApp**

For quick questions and reservations, send us a message on WhatsApp. This is especially convenient for international guests or those who prefer text-based communication.

**Email**

For group bookings, corporate events, or special requests, email us with your details and we'll respond within 24 hours.

📧 [**Contact us →**](/contact)

## What Information We'll Need

When making a reservation, please provide:

1. **Date and time** — Your preferred dining date and arrival time
2. **Number of guests** — How many people will be dining
3. **Name** — The name the reservation will be under
4. **Contact number** — A phone number where we can reach you
5. **Special requests** — Dietary requirements, celebrations, seating preferences

For larger groups (8+), we may ask for additional details to ensure we can accommodate your party comfortably.

## Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both our guests and our kitchen team:

- **More than 24 hours notice** — No charge for cancellation
- **Less than 24 hours notice** — We may request a small deposit for large groups
- **No-show** — For parties of 6 or more, a cancellation fee may apply

If you need to cancel or reschedule, please call us as early as possible. This allows us to offer the table to other guests and adjust our kitchen preparation.

## Group Booking Information

Kumar Restaurant accommodates groups of various sizes, from intimate gatherings to larger celebrations.

**Small Groups (2-6 guests)**

Standard table reservations work perfectly for small groups. We can seat you together at a single table or arrange adjacent tables for your party.

**Medium Groups (7-15 guests)**

For medium groups, we recommend booking at least one week in advance. We can arrange dedicated seating areas and customize set menus for your group.

**Large Groups (16+ guests)**

Large group bookings require advance planning. We'll work with you to determine the best setup, menu options, and timing for your event. [Contact us](/contact) to discuss your needs.

**Corporate and Private Events**

For corporate dining, private events, or special occasions, we offer customized arrangements. Our team can help with menu planning, decoration, and logistics to make your event seamless.

## Day-of Information

When you arrive for your reservation:

**Check In**

Arrive at Act Tower B1 and check in at our reception. Let us know the name your reservation is under, and we'll guide you to your table.

**Arrival Window**

We hold reservations for 15 minutes past the scheduled time. If you'll be more than 15 minutes late, please call us so we can adjust accordingly.

**Seating Preferences**

If you have a preferred seating location—by the window, in a quieter area, near the entrance—let us know when you book. We'll do our best to accommodate your request.

**Dietary Requirements**

If you or your guests have dietary restrictions, allergies, or preferences, inform us when making your reservation. This allows our kitchen to prepare accordingly and ensures a smooth dining experience.

## Tips for the Best Experience

**Come Hungry**

Indian food is meant to be shared and savored. Our portions are generous, and with multiple dishes on the table, you'll want room to explore the full range of flavors.

**Order in Courses**

While there are no strict rules, a typical Indian meal flows naturally from appetizers to main courses to dessert. Our staff can guide you through a suggested ordering progression.

**Try Something New**

If you're a regular, challenge yourself to try a dish you've never ordered before. Our menu has dozens of options, and our staff can suggest dishes based on what you usually enjoy.

**Ask About Specials**

We occasionally feature seasonal specials and limited-time dishes. Ask your server what's new—there might be something you won't want to miss.

**Share Your Experience**

After your visit, we'd love to hear your feedback. Leave a review, share photos, or simply tell a friend about your experience at Kumar Restaurant.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do you accept walk-ins?**

Yes! Walk-ins are welcome during all operating hours. However, we recommend reservations during peak times to avoid waiting.

**Can I modify my reservation?**

Absolutely. Call us to change the date, time, or party size. We're flexible and happy to accommodate changes.

**Do you have parking?**

Act Tower has a parking facility available to restaurant guests. The parking lot is accessible from the building entrance.

**Is the restaurant accessible?**

Kumar Restaurant is located in Act Tower B1. The building has elevator access, and our dining area is accessible for guests with mobility needs.

**Do you offer takeaway?**

Yes! You can order by phone for takeaway pickup. Call [053-451-0154](/contact) to place your order.
